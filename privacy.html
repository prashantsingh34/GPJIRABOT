GPJIRABOT — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-11-16

Privacy Policy

GPJIRABOT respects your privacy. This policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

Information We Collect

  • Messages sent to the bot in Google Chat™
  • Jira issue information returned through the Jira API

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used only to perform actions requested by the user, such as creating or updating Jira tickets.

Data Sharing

We do not share or sell your data to third parties.

Data Storage

No user data is permanently stored by GPJIRABOT.

Security

All communication with Google Chat™ and Jira APIs is encrypted using industry-standard HTTPS.

Contact
